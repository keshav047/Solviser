Services

AI Based Buyer Reputation & Risk Assessment system

The main purpose of the AI based buyer reputation & Risk Assessment system is allow to track buyer reputation, risk and buyers reputation score which will help sellers to know about buyer's business health and analyse the risk to reduce delayed payment.

Smart Contract

The Legal/Smart Contract is allow the buyer and seller to the lock and store their mutually agreed terms and conditions of the deal, which cannot be changed or manipulated once it is digitally signed by both the parties, which could be used as most supportive documents for the legal prospective in future.

Centralized Buyer

The Centralized Buyer Blocklist allows suppliers to block buyers who are involved in a dispute with a seller or do not pay by any excuse. Such a buyer will be put on the blocklist due to which such merchant will not be able to buy goods from any other merchant across India until the previous merchant clears the account or resolves the old issues.

Automated Accounting & Delivery Systems.

Automated accounting systems that allow suppliers to automate their Invoice, quotations, POs, Payment Reminders and Accounting according to Legal/smart contract without any human interface, not only eliminating human errors but also saving vendors time which they would utilise to grow their business and improve quality etc.

E-commerce Page

On E-commerce page allow suppliers/buyers create their own products and service list to show their products across India.

Peer to Peer Internal Supplier Network.

Internal Suppliers Network is help to prevent price fluctuations. In this system, all the sellers of the Industry will be connected to the network and will keep an eye on the daily price fluctuations. Due to this, no trader will be able to sell goods at a lower rate outside his network, In the case of emergency sellers can offer to sale below price to network members only.

About SOLVISER

At SOLVISER, we empower businesses by fostering a trustworthy and secure marketplace. Founded to support MSMEs, our mission is to address core challenges in buyer risk, delayed payments, fraudulent activities, automated accounting, and market uncertainty.

Our platform integrates buyer reputation and risk assessment systems, automated legal support, and centralized buyer block lists, enabling businesses to make informed decisions and safeguard their interests.

We understand the challenges of financial stability, especially for smaller businesses. Our tools for accounting automation, payment reminders, and peer-to-peer supplier networking streamline operations and strengthen business relationships.

By partnering with SOLVISER, you join a network dedicated to reducing non-performing assets, fostering trust, and creating a secure environment for business transactions.

Our Vision

To create a safer, more transparent marketplace that empowers MSMEs and fosters financial health across industries.

Our Mission

To protect businesses from financial risks, including delayed payments, through innovative tools for reputation management, legal support, and market connectivity.
